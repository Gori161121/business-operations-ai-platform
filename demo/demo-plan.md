# Demo Plan

## Demo Goal

The demo will show how the platform can manage business operations, automate workflows, generate reports, and support decision-making.

---

## Demo Scenario

A business manager wants to monitor daily operations, employee shifts, workflow performance, and business KPIs from one dashboard.

---

## Demo Flow

### Step 1 — Business Data Input

Sample business data is entered into the system:

- employees
- shifts
- tasks
- reports
- workflow events

### Step 2 — Database Storage

The system stores structured business information inside the database layer.

### Step 3 — Workflow Automation

Automation workflows process business events such as:

- shift completion
- report generation
- task assignment
- notification triggers

### Step 4 — AI Analysis

The AI Operations Engine analyzes operational data and generates summaries, recommendations, and insights.

### Step 5 — Dashboard Output

The dashboard displays:

- KPIs
- workflow activity
- workforce status
- operational summaries
- AI-generated insights

---

## Demo Outcome

The demo should show how business operations can move from manual tracking to an intelligent, automated, and data-driven system.
