# Workflow Design

## Overview

The Business Operations AI Platform is designed around automated workflows that connect business data, operational processes, AI intelligence, and reporting systems.

The goal is to reduce manual work and create efficient, scalable operations.

---

## Workflow Philosophy

Business processes should not rely on repetitive manual actions.

Every workflow should:

* collect data automatically
* process information automatically
* generate insights automatically
* support business decisions

---
