# Operations Metrics

## Purpose

This document defines the key operational metrics used to measure workforce performance, operational efficiency, revenue generation and business health.

The platform uses these metrics to support reporting, analytics and decision-making.

---

# Revenue Metrics

## Total Revenue

Definition:

Total revenue generated during a reporting period.

Formula:

Total Revenue = Sum of All Product Sales

---

## Company Revenue

Definition:

Revenue allocated to the company after venue share distribution.

Formula:

Company Revenue = Total Revenue × 40%

---

## Venue Revenue

Definition:

Revenue allocated to the venue.

Formula:

Venue Revenue = Total Revenue × 60%

---

# Workforce Metrics

## Worked Hours

Definition:

Total hours completed by workers during a reporting period.

Formula:

Worked Hours = Sum of Shift Hours

---

## Revenue Per Employee

Definition:

Average revenue generated by an employee.

Formula:

Revenue Per Employee =
Total Revenue / Active Employees

---

## Revenue Per Hour

Definition:

Revenue generated for every worked hour.

Formula:

Revenue Per Hour =
Total Revenue / Worked Hours

---

## Workforce Utilization

Definition:

Percentage of available workforce capacity that is actively used.

Formula:

Workforce Utilization =
Worked Hours / Available Hours × 100

Target:

80% – 95%

---

# Shift Metrics

## Total Shifts

Definition:

Total number of completed shifts.

---

## Average Shift Revenue

Definition:

Average revenue generated per shift.

Formula:

Average Shift Revenue =
Total Revenue / Total Shifts

---

## Shift Completion Rate

Definition:

Percentage of scheduled shifts completed successfully.

Formula:

Completed Shifts / Scheduled Shifts × 100

Target:

95%+

---

# Venue Metrics

## Venue Revenue

Definition:

Revenue generated by a specific venue.

---

## Venue Performance Score

Definition:

Revenue efficiency of a venue.

Formula:

Venue Revenue / Venue Shift Count

---

## Revenue Share Contribution

Definition:

Contribution of a venue to total company revenue.

Formula:

Venue Revenue / Total Revenue × 100

---

# Payroll Metrics

## Payroll Cost

Definition:

Total salary paid to workers.

Formula:

Sum of Worker Salaries

---

## Bonus Cost

Definition:

Total bonuses paid during reporting period.

Formula:

Sum of Worker Bonuses

---

## Payroll Efficiency

Definition:

Revenue generated relative to payroll cost.

Formula:

Total Revenue / Payroll Cost

---

# Data Quality Metrics

## Assignment Accuracy

Definition:

Percentage of reports assigned automatically without manual review.

Formula:

Auto Assigned Reports / Total Reports × 100

Target:

90%+

---

## Manual Review Rate

Definition:

Percentage of reports requiring manual review.

Formula:

Manual Reviews / Total Reports × 100

Target:

Below 10%

---

# Analytics Metrics

## Employee Productivity Score

Combines:

- Revenue Per Hour
- Shift Completion
- Revenue Contribution

Used for workforce evaluation.

---

## Location Performance Index

Combines:

- Revenue
- Shift Volume
- Revenue Per Shift
- Workforce Utilization

Used for venue comparison.

---

# AI Metrics

## Recommendation Acceptance Rate

Definition:

Percentage of AI recommendations accepted by management.

Formula:

Accepted Recommendations /
Generated Recommendations × 100

---

## Operational Insight Coverage

Definition:

Percentage of reporting periods that generated AI operational summaries.

Target:

100%
