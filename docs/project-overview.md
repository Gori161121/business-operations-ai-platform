# Project Overview

Business Operations AI Platform is designed as an intelligent system for managing business operations, automation workflows, reporting, and decision support.

The platform connects business data, databases, automation tools, AI assistants, and dashboards into one operational ecosystem.

## Main Purpose

The purpose of this project is to reduce manual work, improve reporting accuracy, and support better business decisions through AI, automation, and data analytics.

## Key Areas

* Business operations
* AI-powered workflow automation
* Employee and shift management
* Revenue and sales tracking
* Reporting and dashboards
* Decision support systems
