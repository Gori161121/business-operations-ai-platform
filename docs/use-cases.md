# Use Cases

## Use Case 1 — Employee Shift Management

A manager needs to track which employees worked, where they worked, and how many hours were completed.

The system stores shift records and provides visibility into workforce activity.

---

## Use Case 2 — Daily Operations Report

At the end of each day, the platform generates an operational summary.

The report includes:

- active employees
- completed shifts
- worked hours
- operational activity
- management recommendations

---

## Use Case 3 — Workforce Analytics

The system analyzes employee workload and shift distribution.

This helps managers identify:

- workload imbalance
- underutilized staff
- high-activity locations
- operational bottlenecks

---

## Use Case 4 — AI Operations Summary

The AI Operations Engine reviews business data and creates a management summary.

Example output:

- key operational highlights
- risks or unusual activity
- performance insights
- recommended next actions

---

## Use Case 5 — Automated Reporting Workflow

A scheduled automation workflow collects data, triggers analytics, and generates a daily report.

This reduces manual reporting work and improves consistency.

---

## Use Case 6 — Executive Decision Support

Executives use dashboards, analytics, and AI-generated insights to make better operational decisions.

The platform supports:

- performance monitoring
- reporting visibility
- workforce planning
- business intelligence
