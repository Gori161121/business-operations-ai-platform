# UI Concept

## Main Interface

The platform interface is designed as an executive business operations dashboard.

## Key Screens

### Executive Dashboard

Shows:

- business KPIs
- operational performance
- workforce status
- workflow activity
- AI-generated insights

### Operations Panel

Used for:

- employee management
- shift management
- task tracking
- workflow monitoring

### AI Assistant Panel

Supports:

- business questions
- operational summaries
- performance analysis
- workflow recommendations

### Reporting Dashboard

Includes:

- KPI reports
- revenue summaries
- workforce reports
- operational analytics

## Design Direction

- clean SaaS dashboard
- dark navy and white interface
- business intelligence style
- executive-level layout
- simple navigation
- data-first structure
