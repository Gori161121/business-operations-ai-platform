# System Flow Diagram

## Business Operations Flow

```text
Business Inputs
      ↓
Data Collection
      ↓
Database Layer
      ↓
Workflow Automation
      ↓
AI Operations Engine
      ↓
Business Intelligence Dashboard
      ↓
Executive Decisions

Layer Explanation
Business Inputs

Operational data such as employees, shifts, tasks, reports, sales, expenses, and workflow events.

Data Collection

Data is collected from forms, Airtable, Supabase, spreadsheets, APIs, and workflow triggers.

Database Layer

Structured data is stored in PostgreSQL, MySQL, Supabase, and Airtable.

Workflow Automation

n8n, Make, APIs, and webhooks automate repetitive operational processes.

AI Operations Engine

AI analyzes operational data, summarizes reports, detects patterns, and supports decision-making.

Business Intelligence Dashboard

Power BI, Tableau, and analytics dashboards provide business visibility.

Executive Decisions

Managers and business owners use insights to improve operations, performance, and strategy.
